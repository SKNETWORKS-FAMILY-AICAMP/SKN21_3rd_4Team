데이터 압축 및 보관
*******************

The modules described in this chapter support data compression with
the zlib, gzip, bzip2, lzma, and zstd algorithms, and the creation of
ZIP- and tar-format archives.  See also 아카이브 연산 provided by the
"shutil" module.
